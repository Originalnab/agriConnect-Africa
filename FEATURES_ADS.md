## AgriConnect Africa – What You Get (Farmers & Buyers)

### For Farmers
- **AI Farm Coach**: Ask questions in English, Twi, Ewe, or Ga and get instant, practical advice.
- **Crop Doctor**: Snap a photo and get AI diagnosis with treatment steps; see common pests/diseases and rotation tips.
- **Planting Calendar**: Know the best time to plant by region and crop.
- **Live Insights Dashboard**: Current weather, pest/disease risk alerts, and market-aware updates.
- **Farming Guides**: Quick, localized guides for key crops (seasons, soil, care, pests, companions).
- **Offline-Friendly**: Caches some insights so you’re not stranded when the signal drops.

### For Buyers
- **Marketplace Access**: Browse farmer listings by crop, price, and location.
- **Supplier Discovery**: View details, locations, and posted quantities to source faster.
- **Posting & Requests**: Farmers list produce; buyers post what they need (UI ready to connect to your messaging flow).
- **Multi-language UI**: Accessible to teams working across Ghanaian languages.

### Shared Benefits
- **AI Visuals**: Generate farming visuals to explain ideas and practices.
- **Voice & Read-Aloud**: Voice input for chat (online) and text-to-speech replies.
- **Mobile-First**: Designed for phone use, adapts to desktop as well.
- **Safe AI Backend**: AI keys stay server-side via a secure proxy—no exposed secrets.
